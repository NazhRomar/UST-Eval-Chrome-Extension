# 🐯 USTe Eval Auto-Filler

Are you too lazy to manually click through 30+ evaluation questions to rate your profs? Has your dean asked you for a follow-up on your pending evaluations? Are you cramming to finish them before the portal closes just so you can finally view your grades?

Say less. **USTe Eval Auto-Filler** is a lightweight, modern Chrome extension that automates the University of Santo Tomas faculty evaluation form in a single click.

## ✨ Features
* **One-Click Magic:** Fills out the entire "Faculty Competence Evaluation Tool" (Part A, B, and C) instantly.
* **Separated Training Needs:** Individually set the score for the "Training Needs Assessment" section at the bottom.
* **Modern UI:** A clean, sleek popup with UST-inspired gold accents.
* **Smart Alerts:** Lets you know exactly how many questions were filled or if you're on the wrong page.

## 🚀 Installation Guide

Since this isn't on the Chrome Web Store, you'll need to install it manually. It takes less than a minute!

1. **Download the code:** Create a new folder on your computer named `UST-Eval-Extension`.
2. **Add the files:** Save the three files provided (`manifest.json`, `popup.html`, and `popup.js`) into this folder.
3. **Open Chrome Extensions:** Open Google Chrome and type `chrome://extensions/` in your URL bar, or go to Settings > Extensions.
4. **Enable Developer Mode:** Toggle the **Developer mode** switch in the top right corner of the screen.
5. **Load the Extension:** Click the **Load unpacked** button in the top left corner.
6. **Select the Folder:** Choose the `UST-Eval-Extension` folder you created in Step 1.
7. **Pin it!** Click the puzzle piece icon in your Chrome toolbar and pin the extension for easy access.

## 🛠️ How to Use

1. Log in to your myUSTe portal and navigate to **Evaluation > College Faculty**.
2. Click on the name of the professor you want to evaluate to open the questionnaire.
3. Click the **USTe Eval Auto-Filler** extension icon in your toolbar.
4. Select your desired rating (1-4) for the main evaluation and the Training Needs Assessment.
5. Click **Autofill Evaluation**.
6. Type your specific rants or praises in the comment boxes (optional but highly recommended for *those* profs).
7. Hit the final **Submit Evaluation** button on the website. Done!

## ⚠️ Disclaimer
This extension was created for convenience and to save time when dealing with a slow portal or repetitive clicking. Please use this responsibly. If a professor actually did a great job (or a terrible one), take a moment to leave them an honest comment!
